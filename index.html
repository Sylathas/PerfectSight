PS004

Palazzi D'Oriente and Michael Mills are set to release their upcoming track "Sleep Bag" on June 29th. Following their previous glitchy single "Baby Kid" the duo returns with a new banger. In "Sleep Bag" the chopped vocal stabs slice through a dense and heavy danceable dembow instrumental, accompanied by hypnotic saw-like synths. The addition of arcade-style arpeggios provides the final touch needed to uplift the dancefloor.

PS003

Baby Kid is the debut single from the collaborative EP by Palazzi D'Oriente and Michael Mills. The song features a vibrant blend of rhythms and sharp synths, taking the listener on a journey through the floors of a weird construction. The synth loop in the bridge adds a playful touch, resembling the sound of an elevator moving up and down.

PS002

Following the sudden and explosive single "Wife Riddim", Palazzi D'Oriente completes the release with two new tracks: Pitch Valley and Wife Dub. Pitch Valley is a deep and meditative movement, that leads to a trippy climax, while Wife Dub brings the previous collaboration with Kamohelo to a minimal instrumental state, creating a perfect tool for DJs and MCs.
